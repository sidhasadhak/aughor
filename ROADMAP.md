## 1 آ· What we set out to build

An **autonomous data-analysis platform** that replaces the dashboard-and-analyst loop. The mandate (see [`README.md`](README.md)):

- **Connect any warehouse and explore it continuously in the background** â€” no prompts, no dashboards to maintain.
- **Build a living business ontology from the data** â€” entities, relationships, metrics, lifecycles â€” with no docs required.
- **Answer hard analytical questions with evidence** â€” citations, real numbers, and statistical confidence; "numbers you can act on," not plausible-looking ones.
- **Know *when* matters** â€” Adaptive Temporal Scope discovers the right window (regime, cost, sufficiency) instead of `MAX(date)`.
- **Surface intelligence at three altitudes** â€” Domains (raw) â†’ Hub (structured) â†’ Briefing (narrative) â€” all over one trusted substrate.
- **Be trustworthy and deployable** â€” deterministic correctness guards, audit/lineage, secrets at rest, capability tiers.

*No dashboards to maintain. No SQL to write. No analyst backlog.*
